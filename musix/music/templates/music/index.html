How it all works?

. . . . . . . . . .

Musix is all about creating a personalised collection of playlists and albums on cloud which can be accesed from anywhere by any device. It allows a user to explore the song collection of other users.

1

What can I do with this web app?

Musix is an app that allows you to upload and store all of your music on the cloud.

You can access your or other users music collection from anywhere through any device.

2

How do I upload my music?

Just register on the website and you will be taken to your profile page.

You can upload all the albums, songs and playlists there.

3

How do I explore other users playlists?

You need to have an account for that.

Log in and then just search the username of the person whose playlist you want to explore.

4

What all audio formats are suppported?

Currently we support only mp3, wav and ogg audio formats.

We will be adding other audio formats soon.
